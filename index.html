Open Online Education Platform.
A group of technologists, students, and academics developing tools to reimagine online education.
Advocating for sharing free course materials
Building open-source edTech
Creating collaborations between institutions
Distributing and collecting resources
We are a group of technologists, students, and faculty growing out of Harvard and MIT, working on online education at the university level. We believe online education naturally lends itself to collaboration and needs further development. OOEP consists of a coalition of projects and partners.
OOEP abides by the following:
We are not for profit
We are inter-university
Our projects are open and freely available
We believe in supporting everyone in online education regardless of whether they abide by these principles. For example, even if you do not publish your course materials, we still want to work with you.
